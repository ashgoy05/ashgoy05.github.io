About This Blog

I'm Ash. I started this blog because most finance content is either generic advice or flexing highlight reels. I wanted something different — a raw, unfiltered account of what it actually looks like to build wealth as a regular person.

The goal is $1,000,000. I invest in US stocks with a heavy thesis on AI infrastructure — the companies building the physical backbone of the AI economy. GEV, AMD, VRT, and CAT are my highest conviction plays.

Every trade I make gets logged here. Every monthly update. Every time I'm wrong. The transparency is the whole point.
